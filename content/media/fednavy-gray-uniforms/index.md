## Description

This artwork was originally created by [Secio](https://twitter.com/scio_12) while under commission from Cliff Ricks. The original artwork was finished on October 23, 2023.

This artwork was then modified by [Wazabii](https://twitter.com/wzb1998) while under commission from Cliff Ricks. The modification includes a redraw of the combination covers (hats). The modified artwork was finalized on November 15, 2023.

This artwork was edited for website publishing by Cliff Ricks on November 17, 2025.
